Sitio Bebe

An area where in people are so happy, hardworking, caring, and kind. Sitio Bebe is currently
facing a severe health crisis, this town is not close to a natural waterway so the local government
often imports clean or portable water for the resident. Most of the population are workers. The common
livelihood of the residents here is mining and factory workers. The area is rich in human resources because
the residents here are hardworking, whether young or old. Sitio Bebe needs to be helped in the crisis they
are going through so that even their health is at stake.

Problem

Not every barangay is full of joy and safety just like Sitio Bebe, because they have to face complicated problems, such as lack of clean water to drink because their land is not close to a water source and due to the high temperature the water that they are stocking is drying up very quickly. And a high number of citizens are sick from hypertension, acute respiratory infection, and acute lower respiratory tract infection.Sitio Bebe is also lacking health professionals, hence the residents are having a hard time looking for health professionals when medical support is needed.

Solution

In order to overcome the problem of Sitio Bebe, we decided to create a program
that invites people to join the concert about the current problem of Sitio Bebe. The
program is located in Quezon City which can hold up to 500 people. We also invited
famous folk pop/pop rock band Ben&Ben to our concert, after the program the money
that has been donated will be invested in water filters, water tanks to make it water clean
for sitio Bebe and drinkable waters and the rest of the money will be given to the hospital
for medical supply and Brgy for future needs.
